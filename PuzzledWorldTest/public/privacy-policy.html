PuzzledWorld Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

PuzzledWorld ("the app," "we," "us") is a jigsaw puzzle app that lets you turn your own photos and public-domain artwork into puzzles. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it's used, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

DataWhenWhy
Email address If you create an account (email/password or Google sign-in) To let you sign in and sync your puzzle progress across sessions
Photos you choose to use When you pick a photo from your device to build a puzzle To generate your puzzle; stored so you can resume an in-progress puzzle
Puzzle activity Automatically, as you use the app To track puzzles you've completed, favorited gallery artwork, and completion stats (time, difficulty, piece count)
Account identifier (user ID) Automatically, if signed in To associate your puzzles and progress with your account internally
Report details Only if you use the "Report this image" feature To review flagged content for policy violations
Advertising ID and device data Automatically, while using the app To serve and measure ads through Google AdMob (see Section 4)

You can also use PuzzledWorld as a guest without creating an account. In guest mode, we do not collect an email address or sync anything to an account, and puzzle progress is not saved between sessions.

2. Photos and Content Moderation

If you choose to share a puzzle you made from your own photo, that image is automatically checked using Google Cloud Vision's SafeSearch service before it's made available via a shareable link, to help prevent sharing of inappropriate or explicit content. This automated check is performed by Google Cloud as our service provider and is not used by Google or anyone else for any purpose beyond that moderation check.

Publicly shared puzzles are only accessible to someone who has the exact share link — they are not listed, searchable, or browsable within the app.

3. How We Store and Protect Your Information

PuzzledWorld uses Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, and Cloud Storage) to store account and puzzle data. Access to your personal puzzle data (in-progress puzzles, completed puzzle history, favorites) is restricted to your account only, enforced by server-side security rules.

4. Third-Party Services

We rely on the following third-party services to operate the app:

  • Google Firebase — authentication, database, file storage, and moderation functions.
  • Google Cloud Vision API — automated image content-safety scanning for shared photos.
  • Google AdMob — shows banner and interstitial ads in the app. AdMob may collect your advertising ID and other device data to serve and measure ads, and to help prevent fraud. See Google's Partner Sites policy for how Google uses this data.

We do not use analytics/tracking SDKs beyond what's described above, and we do not sell any personal information to third parties.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your account and puzzle data for as long as your account is active. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from the app's home screen ("Delete my account," shown when signed in) — this immediately removes your account, saved puzzles, favorites, completed-puzzle history, and any uploaded photos.

If you're unable to use the in-app option, you can instead email us at puzzledworldapp@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete my data" from the email address associated with your account, and we will delete your account and data within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

PuzzledWorld is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Choices

  • You can use the app as a guest without providing an email address.
  • You can sign out at any time from the home screen.
  • You can request access to or deletion of your data by contacting us (see Section 5).

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

9. Contact Us

Questions about this policy or your data? Email us at puzzledworldapp@gmail.com.
